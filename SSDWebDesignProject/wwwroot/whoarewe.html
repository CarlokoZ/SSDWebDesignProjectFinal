Who are We

Little bit About Us

We are a small non for profit society that is trying to make miracles happen for the people who need them the most. The society started in 2011 and is run entirely on a volunteer basis. We collects funds from fundraising events or from personal donations. When we find an individual, family or group of people that are in needed and if the funds are available the society will anonymously donate a sum usually of around $400 to the people. There is no credit given to any individuals within the society and if possible the people receiving the funds won’t even know who the money has come from.
